Frequently asked questions

How to create an account?

You can create an account by clicking on the "Get started" button on the top right corner of the page. You will be redirected to the registration page where you can fill in your details.

How to take a quiz?

Once you have created an account, you can take a quiz by clicking on the "Take a quiz" button on the top right corner of the page. You will be redirected to the quiz page where you can select a quiz to take.

Should be a part of the EMSI university?

Yes, you should be a part of the EMSI Group to take a quiz. this platform is only for EMSI students.

Can I pass the quiz on my phone?

EduQuiz is fully responsive, so you can take the quiz on your phone.

But we recommend you to take the quiz on your laptop or desktop for a better experience.
