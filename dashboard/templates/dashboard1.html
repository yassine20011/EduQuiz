Usage

EduQuiz is a web application that allows teachers to create quizzes and students to take them. It is currently in development.

Create quizzes : Teachers can use EduQuiz to create quizzes for their students. They can create multiple-choice questions, short answer questions, or even essay questions (Max 1000 caracters). The platform will allow them to set time limits for the quizzes, as well as assign a point value to each question.

Take quizzes : Students can use EduQuiz to take quizzes created by their teachers. They can take the quizzes at the time that their teachers assign, and they can see their scores after the approval of the teacher.

Analyze results : Once the quiz time limit has ended, the teacher can access the results on EduQuiz. There are two types of results available: standard and detailed. The standard results will show the overall score and basic statistics, such as the number of correct answers and the percentage of students who passed or failed. The detailed results will provide more in-depth analytics, such as the time each student took to answer each question, the average time per question, and the percentage of students who answered each question correctly. This information can help the teacher to identify specific areas where their students may need additional support and tailor their teaching accordingly.

Some requeriments to create a quiz:

  • Each quiz must have a unique title.
  • You need to create a excel file as the example.
  • Each true answer must add a -v at the end of the answer.
  • You can have multiple true answers.
  • The cell A1 The name must be Question.
  • The cell B1, C1, D1, E1 The name must be a, b, c, d.

Welcome to EduQuiz beta! With EduQuiz, you can easily complete quizzes created by your teacher using your phone or computer.

How to take a quiz? It's easy! Once your teacher creates a quiz, you can access it by navigating to the Available Quizzes section. You can find this section by clicking on your profile photo located on the right-hand side of the screen or by clicking the button below. There, you will see a list of the quizzes your teacher has created, which can be in one of three states: "Not started," "Take Quiz," or "Completed."

How to see your results? Once you complete a quiz, your results will be displayed immediately. You can also review your completed quizzes in the "Completed Quizzes" section. To access this section, click on your profile photo on the right-hand side of the screen and then click on "Completed Quizzes".

Understanding Quiz Statuses

  • Not started: These are quizzes that have not been started yet, meaning you'll need to wait until the quiz is available at the designated start time. You may need to refresh the page to see the quiz.

  • Take Quiz: These are quizzes that are available to take now. Click "Take Quiz" to start the quiz. Note that you can only take a quiz once, and the countdown timer will begin once the quiz is started.

  • Completed: These are quizzes that you have already completed.
